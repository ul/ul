Dark mode⤴

Do I think the dark mode is overrated? I do. Am I going to provide it on my sites and in my software? Yes, it's an excellent litmus test for theming. And when theming is done right, dark mode is easy to support. When it's easy to support, why not do it for the enjoyment of the people who like it then?

This site uses prefers-color-scheme CSS media feature to decide the mode.
