SoundGardenManifesto

It's a by-product of the creative journey. * Creativity blossoms without limit when put in consciously defined constraints. Self-posed restrictions are what helps to maintain focus and embody art artifacts. * Sound Garden's leitmotif constraint is doing everything live and in real-time.
